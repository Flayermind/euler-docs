## Implied Inflation

The EUL distribution is higher in the first two years of the protocolâ€™s launch to hand increased vote weight to those individuals who will play the largest role in shaping its growth and development. The implied annual inflation (as a percentage of total supply) is shown in Figure 1.

After epoch 0, the schedule begins with \~3% annual inflation and changes gradually over time, increasing to a peak of around \~14% after 1.5 years of launch, before tending towards a steady state of 2.718% by 4 years (see Figure 1).
